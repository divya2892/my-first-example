Finacial benefits

Solar panel installations (sometimes referred to as photo voltaic installations) are pretty much maintenance free. So once they are installed, they'll generate electricity for years without any additional investment or maintenance cost.

Environmental benefits

Indeed, solar panel installations have been known to bring energy to areas that would otherwise have to go without - for example mountainous regions in which it would be virtually impossible to lay any length of power cable.And finally - as if any more benefits were needed

A sound investment

Beside the occasional clean, this no-fuss piece of equipment requires very little attending to. There’s no switching off and on, virtually no adjusting, and there’s little or no cost after the initial payment.

Other benefits

Not only do they have to pay this 'generation tariff', they will also pay you for any electricity you generate but don't consume. This export tariff means you effectively get paid twice for the electricity you produce - on top of your bill savings.
